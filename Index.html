Problem Solved by Kēḷvi AI

The Challenge

In India, each school follows a unique blueprint for their exams. This inconsistency creates a significant challenge for students who need tailored practice materials to effectively prepare for their exams. Finding sample papers that align perfectly with their school’s specific blueprint can be time-consuming and often impossible.

Our Solution

Kēḷvi AI addresses this problem by enabling students to create their own sample exam question papers for practice. The app asks for the following data based on the school’s blueprint for the upcoming exams:

  • Total Marks: The overall marks that the exam will be out of.
  • Number of Sections: The different sections in the exam and their respective marks.
  • Prescribed Portions: The specific chapters or units that will be covered in the exam as outlined in the school’s blueprint.

By inputting this information, students can generate sample papers that mirror the structure and content of their actual exams, ensuring they get the most effective practice possible.
